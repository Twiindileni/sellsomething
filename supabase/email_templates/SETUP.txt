HOW TO USE EMAIL TEMPLATES IN SUPABASE
======================================

── Confirm signup ──────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Supabase → Authentication → Email Templates → "Confirm signup"
2. Subject:  Welcome to Sell Something — confirm your email
3. Body:     paste all of confirm_signup.html

── Reset password ──────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Supabase → Authentication → Email Templates → "Reset password"
2. Subject:  Reset your Sell Something password
3. Body:     paste all of reset_password.html

── URL configuration (required) ───────────────────────────────
Authentication → URL Configuration

  Site URL:
    https://www.sellsomething.online

  Redirect URLs:
    https://www.sellsomething.online/**
    https://www.sellsomething.online/auth/callback
    http://localhost:3001/**

Do NOT change {{ .ConfirmationURL }} in either template —
Supabase replaces it with the real link.
